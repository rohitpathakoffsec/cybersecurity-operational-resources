# URL Check

Tools and platforms used to analyze URLs for malicious behavior, reputation,
redirect chains, hosting details, and threat intelligence enrichment.

## Tools

- VirusTotal – https://www.virustotal.com
- AlienVault OTX – https://otx.alienvault.com/browse/global/indicators
- SecurityTrails (DNS & Infrastructure Data) – https://securitytrails.com/domain/google.com/dns
- URLHaus – https://urlhaus.abuse.ch/browse/
- URLScan – https://urlscan.io/
- IPQualityScore (Malicious URL Scanner) – https://www.ipqualityscore.com/threat-feeds/malicious-url-scanner
- Sucuri SiteCheck – https://sitecheck.sucuri.net/
- InQuest Labs (IOC Database) – https://labs.inquest.net/iocdb
- ThreatFox – https://threatfox.abuse.ch/browse/
- MalwareURL – https://www.malwareurl.com/listing-urls.php
- ThreatMiner – https://www.threatminer.org/
- Pulsedive – https://pulsedive.com/
- WhereGoes (Redirect Tracing) – https://wheregoes.com/
- Redirect Detective – https://redirectdetective.com/
- Redirect Tracker – https://www.redirecttracker.com/
- BulkBlacklist – https://www.bulkblacklist.com/
- DocGuard – https://app.docguard.io/
- IOC.One – https://ioc.one/
