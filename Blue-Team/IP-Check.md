# IP Check

Tools and platforms used to analyze IP addresses for reputation, malicious activity,
VPN/proxy usage, and threat intelligence enrichment.

## Tools

- AlienVault OTX – https://otx.alienvault.com/browse/global/indicators
- GreyNoise (VPN & Noise Detection) – https://viz.greynoise.io
- ThreatFox – https://threatfox.abuse.ch/browse/
- IPQualityScore (VPN / Proxy Detection) – https://www.ipqualityscore.com/free-ip-lookup-proxy-vpn-test
- Shodan – https://www.shodan.io/
- Censys – https://censys.io/ipv4
- Cisco Talos Intelligence – https://talosintelligence.com/
- AbuseIPDB – https://www.abuseipdb.com/
- WhatIsMyIPAddress (Blacklist Check) – https://whatismyipaddress.com/blacklist-check
- The Anti-Abuse Project (Multi-RBL Check) – http://www.anti-abuse.org/multi-rbl-check/
- InQuest Labs (Reputation DB) – https://labs.inquest.net/repdb
- MalwareURL – https://www.malwareurl.com/listing-urls.php
- ThreatMiner – https://www.threatminer.org/ *(currently unavailable)*
- IPinfo – https://ipinfo.io/
- BrowserLeaks – https://browserleaks.com
- IPify VPN & Proxy Detection – https://vpn-proxy-detection.ipify.org/
- IP Teoh (VPN Detection) – https://ip.teoh.io/vpn-detection
- VPNAPI.io (VPN Detection) – https://vpnapi.io/vpn-detection
- Pulsedive – https://pulsedive.com/
- IOC.One – https://ioc.one/
